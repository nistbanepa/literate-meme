Himalayan International Academy

History

The Himalayan International academy was established in 2052 B.S. since its established year is continuously providing quality education with updated technology to Students and parents for development of educational sector in the society.

In 2068 to 2069 school was unable to run school due to some technical & terrible situation. In 2070 B.S it was reopened with complete new management & founders. gradually in very short period school has achieved great result & popularity in community from young and energetic team of school.

Himalayan International Academy aims at imparting quality educating to children consciously about their psychological and physical needs. Its aim is to make possible them to develop their faculties, to strengthen and expand educational schemes for meeting the future challenges in this competitive world.

Vision

The vision of The Himalayan International Academy is provide Scientific, Practical Knowledge & Quality Education that helps to all students will become life long learners equipped with the skills, knowledge and attitudes to succeed as productive citizens in a local, national and global society.

Mission

• To become partner with the community
• To provide an effective educational experience
• To prepare every student to find success in competitive world.
• To prepare every student to be socially responsible with the help of new updated technology , effective & practical educational system.
